What DotMD does

Google-Docs-grade review — built for Markdown.

Full-fidelity rendering

GFM tables, code blocks, Mermaid diagrams, and math render exactly as written — no broken previews, no surprises.

Inline comments

Comment on any selection — a word, a sentence, a whole section — in threaded discussions with @mentions and resolve.

Suggested edits

Propose changes track-changes style. Authors accept or reject line by line, with the full history preserved.

Highlights & annotations

Mark up drafts with color-coded highlights so feedback is impossible to miss and easy to act on.

Real-time collaboration

CRDT-powered editing for 25+ people at once — live cursors, instant sync, and zero merge conflicts.

AI participant mode

Let AI agents join as collaborators. They read, comment, and suggest edits through the API, right alongside your team.

Version history & diff

Every change is tracked. Compare versions side-by-side or inline, and roll back to any point anytime.

Sharing & roles

Share a link with role-based access — viewer, commenter, or editor — and stay in control of every document.

Import, export & git sync

Bring in or ship out .md, .docx, and .pdf, and sync bi-directionally with GitHub and GitLab.

How it works

Three steps from draft to done.

01

Bring it in

Paste Markdown, import .md or .docx, or sync a file straight from GitHub or GitLab.

02

Review together

Humans and AI comment, suggest edits, and highlight in real time — threaded, resolvable, and fully tracked.

03

Resolve & ship

Accept changes, check the version diff, then export or sync back to git — formatting and history intact.
